## Team GT 

![](../images/gt.jpg)

### Theme Description
- Any character who made significant contribution to the story of DragonBall GT.


### Team restrictions:
  -  GT Goku and SS4 Vegeta have been modded to be unable to fuse in SS4 Gogeta.

- GT Goku (SSJ1)

- SS4 Vegeta

- Syn Shenron
